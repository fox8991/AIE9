# 2-Week Exercise Program for Alex

## Overview
- **Duration:** 2 weeks
- **Frequency:** 3 sessions per week
- **Duration per session:** 30 minutes
- **Goals:** Improve energy levels, support better sleep, and manage anxiety.
- **Level:** Beginner to intermediate fitness level

Each session will include:
1. Warm-up (5 minutes)
2. Main Workout (20 minutes)
3. Cool-down (5 minutes)

---

## Week 1

### Session 1 (Monday)
**Warm-up (5 minutes):**  
- Arm Circles: 1 minute (30 seconds forward, 30 seconds backward)  
- Leg Swings: 1 minute (30 seconds each leg)  
- Marching in place: 2 minutes  
- Side bends: 1 minute (30 seconds each side)  

**Main Workout (20 minutes):**  
- Bodyweight Squats: 3 sets x 10 reps (modification: reduce depth if needed)  
- Push-ups (on knees if needed): 3 sets x 5-8 reps  
- Standing Overhead Dumbbell Press (2-5 lbs): 3 sets x 10 reps  
- Glute Bridges: 3 sets x 10 reps  
- Plank (on knees if needed): 3 sets x 15 seconds  

**Cool-down (5 minutes):**  
- Seated Forward Bend: 1 minute  
- Child’s Pose: 1 minute  
- Deep Breathing: 3 minutes  

---

### Session 2 (Wednesday)
**Warm-up (5 minutes):**  
- Arm Swings: 1 minute  
- High Knees: 2 minutes  
- Torso Twists: 2 minutes  

**Main Workout (20 minutes):**  
- Lunges (stationary): 3 sets x 8 reps each leg (modification: reduce range of motion)  
- Dumbbell Rows (2-5 lbs): 3 sets x 10 reps  
- Wall Sits: 3 sets x 20 seconds  
- Bicycle Crunches: 3 sets x 10 reps each side  
- Side Plank (on knees if needed): 3 sets x 10 seconds each side  

**Cool-down (5 minutes):**  
- Quadriceps Stretch: 1 minute each leg  
- Shoulder Stretch: 1 minute each side  
- Deep Breathing: 3 minutes  

---

### Session 3 (Friday)
**Warm-up (5 minutes):**  
- Neck Rolls: 1 minute  
- Dynamic Hamstring Stretch: 2 minutes (1 minute each leg)  
- Side Lunges: 2 minutes  

**Main Workout (20 minutes):**  
- Step-ups (onto a sturdy surface): 3 sets x 8 reps each leg  
- Incline Push-ups: 3 sets x 5-8 reps  
- Dumbbell Deadlifts (2-5 lbs): 3 sets x 10 reps  
- Seated Russian Twists (bodyweight or light dumbbell): 3 sets x 10 reps each side  
- Cat-Cow Stretch: 3 sets x 5 reps  

**Cool-down (5 minutes):**  
- Butterfly Stretch: 2 minutes  
- Neck Stretch: 1 minute each side  
- Deep Breathing: 1 minute  

---

## Week 2

### Session 4 (Monday)
**Warm-up (5 minutes):**  
- Arm Circles: 1 minute  
- Leg Swings: 1 minute  
- Jumping Jacks (low impact if needed): 2 minutes  
- Side bends: 1 minute  

**Main Workout (20 minutes):**  
- Bodyweight Squats: 4 sets x 12 reps  
- Push-ups: 4 sets x 6-10 reps (mix knee and standard)  
- Standing Overhead Dumbbell Press: 4 sets x 12 reps  
- Glute Bridges: 4 sets x 12 reps  
- Plank: 4 sets x 20 seconds  

**Cool-down (5 minutes):**  
- Seated Forward Bend: 1 minute  
- Child’s Pose: 1 minute  
- Deep Breathing: 3 minutes  

---

### Session 5 (Wednesday)
**Warm-up (5 minutes):**  
- Arm Swings: 1 minute  
- High Knees: 2 minutes  
- Torso Twists: 2 minutes  

**Main Workout (20 minutes):**  
- Lunges: 4 sets x 10 reps each leg  
- Dumbbell Rows: 4 sets x 12 reps  
- Wall Sits: 4 sets x 30 seconds  
- Bicycle Crunches: 4 sets x 12 reps each side  
- Side Plank: 4 sets x 15 seconds each side  

**Cool-down (5 minutes):**  
- Quadriceps Stretch: 1 minute each leg  
- Shoulder Stretch: 1 minute each side  
- Deep Breathing: 3 minutes  

---

### Session 6 (Friday)
**Warm-up (5 minutes):**  
- Neck Rolls: 1 minute  
- Dynamic Hamstring Stretch: 2 minutes  
- Side Lunges: 2 minutes  

**Main Workout (20 minutes):**  
- Step-ups: 4 sets x 10 reps each leg  
- Incline Push-ups: 4 sets x 8-10 reps  
- Dumbbell Deadlifts: 4 sets x 12 reps  
- Seated Russian Twists: 4 sets x 12 reps each side  
- Cat-Cow Stretch: 4 sets x 6 reps  

**Cool-down (5 minutes):**  
- Butterfly Stretch: 2 minutes  
- Neck Stretch: 1 minute each side  
- Deep Breathing: 1 minute  

---

## Notes for Success
1. **Listen to Your Body:** Modify exercises as needed to match your fitness level and comfort.
2. **Stay Hydrated:** Drink water before, during, and after your workouts.
3. **Consistency is Key:** Stick to the schedule as much as possible.
4. **Focus on Breathing:** During both workouts and while cooling down, focus on your breathing to help manage anxiety and promote relaxation.

---

## Reminder
Set alarms to ensure morning workouts fit into your schedule. Enjoy your workouts!
