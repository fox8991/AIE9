# Alex's 2-Week Vegetarian Nutrition Plan

## Overview
This comprehensive meal plan is designed to:
- **Improve energy levels** throughout the day
- **Support better sleep quality** 
- **Help manage mild anxiety** through mindful food choices
- **Provide complete vegetarian nutrition**

---

## General Nutrition Tips for Sustained Energy and Better Sleep

### Energy Optimization
- **Stay Hydrated**: Drink plenty of water throughout the day to maintain energy levels
- **Eat Regularly**: Aim for meals every 3-4 hours to keep energy levels stable
- **Balanced Meals**: Include complex carbohydrates, protein, and healthy fats in every meal
- **Iron-Rich Foods**: Include spinach, lentils, and fortified cereals to prevent fatigue

### Sleep Support
- **Limit Caffeine**: Especially after 2 PM, as it can disturb sleep
- **Include Relaxing Foods**: Foods high in magnesium (leafy greens, nuts) and tryptophan (beans, seeds)
- **Timing Matters**: Eat your largest meal at lunch, lighter dinner 3 hours before bed

### Anxiety Management
- **Omega-3 Rich Foods**: Walnuts, chia seeds, flaxseeds for brain health
- **Complex Carbohydrates**: Oats, quinoa, sweet potatoes for steady blood sugar
- **Mindful Eating**: Take time to eat without distractions to reduce stress

## Meal Prep Tips
1. **Sunday Prep**: Make overnight oats, chia puddings, and wash/chop vegetables
2. **Batch Cooking**: Make large portions of soups, stews, and grain salads
3. **Snack Prep**: Portion nuts, cut vegetables, and prepare hummus containers
4. **Protein Prep**: Cook tofu, hard-boil eggs, and prepare legume salads in advance

## Hydration Schedule
- **Morning**: 1-2 glasses of water upon waking
- **Pre-meal**: 1 glass of water 30 minutes before each meal
- **Throughout day**: Aim for 8-10 glasses total
- **Evening**: Herbal tea (chamomile, passionflower) for better sleep

## Special Considerations for Mild Anxiety
- **Avoid**: Excessive caffeine, sugar spikes, skipping meals
- **Include**: Magnesium-rich foods (dark leafy greens, nuts, seeds)
- **Timing**: Consistent meal times to maintain stable blood sugar
- **Mindfulness**: Practice gratitude before meals, eat slowly
